Plate I · The Creatures of the Market

Money is a wild thing.
Learn to read its tracks.

Most guides treat the markets like a machine — cold, technical, joyless. We treat them like a living wilderness, full of curious creatures with names, habits, and moods worth knowing.

Each week we catalogue a new specimen and pin it to the page, so that the ordinary explorer might wander these woods without fear.
