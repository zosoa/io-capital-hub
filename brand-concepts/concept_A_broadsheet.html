Your savings are quietly losing a race you didn't know you'd entered.

By the FinkSmart Editorial Desk · 7 minute read

For a generation taught that a bank account is the responsible choice, the arithmetic has quietly turned. While cash sits still, the cost of living moves forward at a steady three percent a year — and over a decade, that gap compounds into a chasm.

This week's report unpacks the chase in three illustrated charts, then asks the only question that matters: where should a beginner actually begin?
